DEDICATED COLLECTION

Indian Granites

Igneous, hard, and eternal. A premium collection of highly durable natural granites for architectural kitchen countertops and cladding.

Wholesale Indian Granite Supplier

India produces some of the hardest and most beautiful granites in the world. Sunrise Natural Stones supplies premium granite slabs, tiles, and raw blocks quarried from selected deposits in Rajasthan and South India. Our inventory spans deep black granites, classic Sirohi P White, and the highly coveted Alaska and Blue Dunes series.

Granite's high density, heat resistance, and durability make it the ideal material for high-traffic flooring, kitchen countertops, and external building facades. We offer custom calibration, edge profiling, and packaging under strict quality control.

KNOWLEDGE BASE

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What are the common finishes available for exported granites?

We offer high-gloss polished, low-sheen honed, textured leathered, and slip-resistant shot-blast or flamed finishes, depending on your architectural application.

Q. Are your granites sealed before shipping?

We can pre-apply high-grade commercial sealants upon request to simplify post-installation maintenance for your client.

Q. What size options do you offer for granite?

We supply random gang-sawn slabs (usually 2cm or 3cm thickness), cutter slabs, standard tiles (60x60, 60x30 cm), and custom cut-to-size formats.
