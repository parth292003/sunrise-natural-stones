— 01 / About

Exporters of Indian
natural stone.

Sunrise Natural Stones is an exporter of high-quality marble, granite, quartz and sandstone, headquartered in Udaipur, Rajasthan. For more than twenty-five years, we have supplied stone from Indian quarries to projects around the world.

We take great pride in the quality of our stones and would love the opportunity to provide you with the materials you need for your upcoming projects. Whether you are an architect specifying a single statement slab, a fabricator pricing a container, or a developer staging a full façade — we operate at every scale, with the same care.

Our work spans the entire pipeline: selection at the quarry, calibration and finishing under our supervision, packing and logistics. The promise is continuity — the same colour, the same vein direction, the same finish, delivered consistently across multiple shipments.
